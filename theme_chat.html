Instructions

If you stream on Kick from OBS like the above screenshot, you can easily generate "Custom CSS" from below on this page to copy and paste in the box in the place pictured in the screenshot above to theme your on-screen chat and make it look pretty or however you want according to your brand. You would put the browser source website to something like: https://kick.com/sethdrums/chatroom . You get this unique link per channel by clicking on the "Pop Out Chat" option on your current live-stream.
